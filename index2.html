Applications

Solitonik is the first consumer product for mechanical measurements. The ultimate tool to test quality of materials, to monitor drying, curing or changing mechanical properties and aging of mechanical components. Anticipate the breaking of mechanical parts in your car, in your lawn mower, test your insulating properties of your shoes, test your wall before placing a nail, insure the quality of your latest second-hand acquisition, test the curing of your concrete while making home renovation....

Product development and ordering

After 5 years of research and 6 months of product development, we are proud to introduce Solitonik CD, the new consumer device for mechanical property measurements. First orders will be shipped in Spring 2014.

Professional applications

Solitonik is not just a consumer product. We offer high end probes for the construction industry, for research in the medical field of osteoporosis and for the carbon fiber industry.

Technology

Solitonik is introducing the first portable device to measure mechanical properties in-situ. The technology is based on a "non-destructive indentation" of material. Contrary to usual professional techniques, it requires no clamps or precise load frame and no positioning devices, making it the perfect solution for your personal applications.
