Getting manufacturing LEAN

Solitonik develops probes adapted to your manufacturing process to control parts within your process flow. We monitor delamination, low stiffness regions or any mechanical defects. Our commitment is to:

  • Make it easy and fast, to limit time spent by operators or costly training charges
  • Make it cheap, because your profitability is our priority
  • Make it tracable, to inspire trust among your clients
  • Make it robust, to fit your needs and process

Benefits

  • Track back problems in your manufacturing process
  • Gain competitive advantage with unique quality control (tracable and photo-tied in-situ mecanical testing)
