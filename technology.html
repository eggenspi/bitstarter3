Our technology

In situ-stiffness monitoring

Delamination, curing or glueing defects, damages during handling and assembling are some of the many defects that might affect your parts. By monitoring in-situ their mechanical properties, you can detect most of those defects. Solitonik has develop the first device to make in-situ measurements. We know production constraints, so we don't just take procise measurements: we make them fast (10-50 measurements per seconds), we record them to keep tracability and we don't require costly training of operators.

The technology
